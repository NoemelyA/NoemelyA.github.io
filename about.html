A B O U T

I was born in Dominican Republic on November, 7th,1995. At the age of three my family immigrated
to the United States of America. Growing up I lived in Brooklyn, NY, Stratford, CT, and where I currently call
home Pembroke Pines, Florida. I remember growing up I would spend summers with my aunt just watching her
paint her breadthtaking observation oil paintings I was alwasy inspired by her dedication, her creativity,
and how she mastered her craft.So at the age of 13 during my freshman year, I began taking art classes in
my high school. From that year forward I never stopped doing art. My art teacher in high school saw potential
in me, that I did not see in myself.

Like a lot of us, I developed other passions as I grew up and one of these is teaching elementary aged children.
Initially I pursued a career in Elementary Education but soon realized that my true passion which I would have no
problem doing for the rest of my life was art. So I decided to begin a career in graphic design. So far, I have
never regreted my decision. I love being a student learning to do what I LOVE.
